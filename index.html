About Me

Hello and welcome to Kalki Events I’m Shiva Kumar, the proud founder of this venture. With a passion for both design and event management, I’ve dedicated myself to crafting unforgettable spaces and experiences. My journey began with a love for creating environments that not only look stunning but also feel truly special. Over the years, I’ve honed my skills in event planning, ensuring every detail is perfect and every moment memorable. At Kalki Events, I bring that same meticulous attention to detail and creativity to every project.
